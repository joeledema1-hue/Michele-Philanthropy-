About Michele

A Heart for
Those Who Need It Most

"Every person, regardless of ability, deserves to live with dignity, joy, and equal opportunity."

Michele Morrone, the acclaimed Italian actor and global star, has devoted a significant part of his life to advocating for individuals with physical and cognitive disabilities. Beyond the spotlight, Michele walks alongside communities that are often overlooked.

The Michele Philanthropy foundation was born from a deeply personal conviction that fame carries responsibility ‚Äî and that responsibility is to give back, uplift, and empower those who need it most.

From funding accessibility programs to visiting rehabilitation centers across Europe, Michele's commitment is hands-on, heartfelt, and lasting.

What We Do

Our Programs

We run targeted initiatives designed to make a real, measurable difference in the lives of people living with disabilities across Europe and beyond.

Accessibility & Mobility

Providing wheelchairs, mobility aids, and funding accessibility renovations in homes and public spaces so individuals can move freely and independently.

Ongoing

Mental Health Support

Connecting people with disabilities to mental health professionals, peer support groups, and therapy programs to nurture emotional wellbeing.

Active

Scholarships & Education

Funding educational opportunities and vocational training for young people with disabilities so they can build fulfilling, independent futures.

Active

Medical Missions

Organizing medical missions that bring healthcare professionals directly to underserved communities to provide specialized disability care.

Seasonal

Assisted Living

Supporting families and care facilities that provide safe, dignified, and compassionate living environments for individuals with severe disabilities.

Ongoing

Arts & Expression

Running art, music, and creative therapy programs that give people with disabilities a powerful voice and platform for self-expression.
